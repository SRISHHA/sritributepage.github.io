MOTHER TERESA

APOSTLE OF LOVE

This page is dedicated to Mother Teresa of Calcutta, the Catholic nun and saint.A small tribute to her for her tireless services.

  • Birth: 08/26/1910

  • Mother Teresa was born in Macedonnia

  • Baptised: 08/27/1910

  • First Call to Religious Life: 1922 - 1923

  • Became a Roman Catholic Nun (Sister Teresa): 09/25/1928

  • Becomes a Teacher After Moving to Kolkata: 1929

  • Takes Final Vows of a Nun, Becomes Mother Teresa: 1937

  • Call to serve the poor: 1946

  • Founded Missionaries of Charity: 7/10/1950

  • Begins Work with Lepers: 1957

  • Receives Popes Peace Prize: 1971

  • Wins Nobel Peace Prize: 1979

  • Awarded with the Bharat Ratna: March, 1980

  • Rescues 37 Disabled Children: 1982

  • Receives Medal of Freedom (Highest US Honor): 1985

  • Death 5/9/1997
  • Famous quote:Spread love everywhere you go. Let no one ever come to you without leaving happier.
